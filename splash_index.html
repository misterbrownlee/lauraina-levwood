 Lauraina brings nine years of experience as a birth advocate, prenatal & postpartum massage therapist, birth & postpartum doula, infant massage educator, and Birthing From Within mentor. She has a background in dance, philosophy, and creative writing, and her studies of the body and the heart greatly inform her approach as a care provider. Lauraina believes that birth can and should be supported with love, and that when this happens mothers and their partners are more relaxed, endure fewer unnecessary struggles, and feel more powerful and confident as they transition into new parenthood.

To schedule a free 30-minute Nurtured Birth phone consultation, for private birth mentorship, or to make a bodywork appointment, email Lauraina using the form below.
